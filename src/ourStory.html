Our story
Learn about our company and missions
We are passionate about creating innovative products and services to empower athletes everywhere. Our mission is to solve one of the biggest problems paddler athletes face each winter: no water-like indoor paddling.

It all began when our founder József was dreaming of an affordable ergometer that best reflects the workout on the water. The machines available then were either expensive or were not providing the same quality training and were also voluminous. So he decided to design and build for himself a machine that is affordable, compact and more likely to real kayaking. The rest is a history – and we all would be glad if you could be part of.

Everything started when our CEO Jozsef Weimper who is a kayaker, saw himself facing the same problem many athletes face when winter comes: how to have a “water-like” feeling training indoors. He was aware of some machines in the market for this kind if training, but not just they were expensive, they couldn’t provide very big.
